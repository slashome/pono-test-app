he climbs up once more, draws a little nearer. More sudden flights,
followed by fresh approaches, each time nigher than before. This
restless running to and fro is the declaration of the enamoured
swain. Perseverance spells success. The pair are now face to face, she
motionless and grave, he all excitement. With the tip of his leg, he
ventures to touch the plump wench. He has gone too far, daring youth
that he is! Panic-stricken, he takes a header, hanging by his
safety-line. It is only for a moment, however.
