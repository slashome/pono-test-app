Let us leave the beast alone. Chance, a poor stand-by, sometimes contrives very well. At the
beginning of the month of August, the children call me to the far side
of the enclosure, rejoicing in a find which they have made under the
rosemary-bushes. It is a magnificent Lycosa, with an enormous belly, the
sign of an impending delivery. The obese Spider is gravely devouring something in the midst of a
circle of onlookers. And what?
