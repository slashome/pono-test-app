learns his profession from the chief of the robbers. The chief sends him

travellers lie down and then jumps upon them, but does not rob them. The
second time the chief tells him to take the travellers' quattrini (the
name of a very small coin, by which money in general is also expressed). The young thief takes the quattrini alone, and lets the travellers keep
their dollars and napoleons. At last, however, he becomes an
