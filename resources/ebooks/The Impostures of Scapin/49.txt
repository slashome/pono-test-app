Certainly; we have something to settle first of all. LEA. But what? GER. Just stand there before me, and let me look at you. LEA. What for? GER. Look me straight in the face. LEA. Well? GER. Will you tell me what has taken place here in my absence? LEA. What has taken place? GER. Yes; what did you do while I was away? LEA. What would you have me do, father? GER. It is not I who wanted you to do anything, but who ask you now
what it is you did? LEA. I have done nothing to give you reason to complain. GER. Nothing at all? LEA.
