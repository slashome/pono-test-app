Ah, here you are! LEA. (going quickly towards his father to embrace him). Ah! father, how glad I am to see you! GER. (refusing to embrace him). Stay, I have to speak to you
first. LEA. Allow me to embrace you, and.... GER. (refusing him again). Gently, I tell you. LEA. How! father, you deprive me of the pleasure of showing you my
joy at your return? GER.
