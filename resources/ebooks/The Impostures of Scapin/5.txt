And I no less. OCT. When my father hears what has taken place, a storm of reprimands
will burst upon me. SIL. Reprimands are not very heavy to bear; would to heaven I were
free at that price! But I am very likely to pay dearly for all your wild
doings, and I see a storm of blows ready to burst upon my shoulders. OCT. Heavens! how am I to get clear of all the difficulties that
beset my path! SIL.
