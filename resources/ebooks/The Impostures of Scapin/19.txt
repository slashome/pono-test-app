OCT. Any other person would have seemed frightful in the condition
she was in, for all the dress she had on was a scanty old petticoat,
with a night jacket of plain fustian, and turned back at the top of her
head a yellow cap, which let her hair fall in disorder on her shoulders;
and yet dressed even thus she shone with a thousand attractions, and all
her person was most charming and pleasant. SCA.
