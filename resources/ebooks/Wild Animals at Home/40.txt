THE
SHRUNKEN RANGE

It is not generally known that the American Buffalo ranged as far
east as Syracuse, Washington City, and Carolina, that they populated the
forests in small numbers, as well as the plains in great herds. I
estimate them at over 50,000,000 in A.D.
